Constituent Assembly & Committees

📌 Key Facts

  • Formation: Under Cabinet Mission Plan (1946)
  • First Meeting: December 9, 1946
  • Time Taken: 2 years, 11 months, and 18 days
  • Total Sessions: 11 sessions over 165 days
  • Adoption Date: November 26, 1949

🏛️ Major Committees

  • Drafting Committee: Dr. B.R. Ambedkar (7 members)
  • Union Power Committee: Jawaharlal Nehru
  • Provincial Constitution: Sardar Vallabhbhai Patel
  • Fundamental Rights: Sardar Vallabhbhai Patel
  • Rules of Procedure: Dr. Rajendra Prasad
